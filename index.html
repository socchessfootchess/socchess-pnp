Print tips

  • Print PDFs at 100% / Actual size, letter paper, portrait.
  • Trim on crop marks and tape tiles using the overlap.
  • Use cardstock for tokens; a 1" circle punch works great.

Quick rules

  • Teams: 11 pieces: GK + 10 field pieces (Q/R/B/N). For field pieces there must be 2–3 of each.
  • Kickoff / After every goal: Forward Rule — Ball‑holder picks F (0–10) and every field piece moves straight forward F squares; opponent then picks D (0–10) and does the same. GKs don’t move. Resolve landings by rank: GK/K>Q>R>B>N (loser displaces to an adjacent empty square).
  • Score by shots only. Carrying into goal doesn’t score.
  • Actions cadence: 1,1,2,2,3,3,2,1,2,3 (15s per action).
  • Carry: Q/R/B up to 5; GK/N unlimited. Pass: Q/R/B any distance on clear lines; N one L‑jump; GK one square. Shoot along shooter’s move; shooter stays.
  • Challenges: enter holder’s square; rank wins. Blocks: any piece on shot path stops; GK on a block may pass anywhere.
